We Have Network Of Nenasala Center

Nenasala (Wisdom Outlet) is a telecentre project by the Government of Sri Lanka. Developed under the e-Sri Lanka Initiative which is implemented by the ICT Agency of Sri Lanka. Communication centers are being built by the government in rural areas to help fight poverty, develop culture and commerce, and sustain peace. There are currently 751 such centers in the country,[2] with the most recent being established in the Kuliyapitiya Technical college. However the number of e-Nenasala centers was expected to extend up to 1000 centers by the end of the year 2010
